Cosplay Creation Basics

Basic Steps to creating a cosplay prop with EVA foam.
The first step is to create a template for your piece. Trace over an image of the piece, outlining the seperate pieces and print it out.
Cut your EVA foam and sand them down.
Glue together the EVA foam.
To seal it spray paint it with Plastic Dip.
Paint with acrylics.
Seal with spray paint sealer.
